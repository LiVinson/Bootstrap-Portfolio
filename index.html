About Me

I am a native Floridian, born and raised in Orlando. After graduating from the University of Florida, I briefly moved to Baton Rouge, LA where I taught high school math before relocating to Charlotte, NC. While there, I worked in HR as a recruiting coordinator and client delivery manager. I recently relocated back to Orlando to spend time with family.
