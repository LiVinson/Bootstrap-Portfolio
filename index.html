About Me

I am a native Floridian, born and raised in Orlando. After graduating from the University of Florida, I briefly moved to Baton Rouge, LA where I worked as high school teacher, teaching Geometry and Financial Math. In 2013, I relocated to Charlotte, NC. While there, I worked in human resources as a recruiting coordinator and later as a client delivery manager.

I recently relocated back to Orlando to spend time with family and to study to become a full stack web developer. In my free time, I enjoy my fair share of both outdoor and indoor activities. I love to hike, kayak, and take road trips to any place with delicious food (so far, St. Louis has the best barbecue, in my opinion). I also enjoy playing flag football, where I usually play receiver and cornerback. I also enjoy weekend binge watching sessions. Currentl, I am making my way through a list of some of the top anime shows.
